Who We Are

What started as a passion for beautiful details and meaningful gifts… has now become a brand. Curated Crates is a reservoir of cherishing every moment with your family, friends, and corporate connections — reaffirming the importance of relationships.

We recognized a gap in the market for curated, meaningful gifts that reflect a true personal touch. That's how Curated Crates came into existence — combining expert selection and personalization to deliver beautiful hampers that create lasting memories.

Our Purpose

With people giving and receiving generic gifts, the gifting gesture has become somewhat monotonous. We, at Curated Crates, aim to redefine the gifting landscape with a wide range of personalized, premium, and delightful gift options.

Our goal is to make the gifting experience unforgettable — for both the person giving the gift and the one receiving it. We lovingly craft every hamper to match unique personalities, relationships, and occasions.

Meet the Founder

Shreya Gandhi

“In a world that moves fast, I wanted to slow things down — to bring back the charm of thoughtful gestures and beautifully crafted moments. Curated Crates is more than just a gifting brand; it's a celebration of stories, relationships, and the art of making someone feel special. Every hamper is my love letter to joy, beauty, and personal connection.”
